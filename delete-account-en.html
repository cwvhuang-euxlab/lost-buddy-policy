Lost Buddy Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Welcome to Lost Buddy (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We are fully committed to protecting your privacy and data security. In compliance with App Store and Google Play developer policies, we provide a transparent and clear account deletion mechanism. You may request the deletion of your account and associated personal identifiers at any time.

1. How to Request Account Deletion In-App

Registered users can easily initiate the deletion process directly within the App. The operational path is as follows:

  1. Open the Lost Buddy App and ensure you are logged in.
  2. Navigate to your "Profile Page" from the main navigation menu.
  3. Tap the "Settings" icon located at the top-right corner of the screen to enter the page titled "Settings".
  4. Select the "Delete Account" option from the settings list.
  5. Read the confirmation prompt, and tap confirm to submit your deletion request.

2. Scope of Data Deletion & Processing Timeline

To ensure your privacy rights are strictly safeguarded, once a formal account deletion request is submitted, our backend system will execute the following cleanup and anonymization protocols:

  1. Eradication of Personal Information: Your core personal identifiable information, including third-party authentication credentials (such as Google or Apple sign-in identifiers), registered email addresses, and profile picture URLs, will be completely permanently purged or covered within 7 business days. Once processed, the account will be rendered permanently inaccessible.
  2. Retention Policy for Historical Public Reports: To maintain the integrity of our community's historical search records and protect public interests, lost pet notices or sighting reports you previously published on the public map will not be forcefully removed. Instead, the author identity linked to those cases will be automatically converted to "Lost Buddy User". This completely severs all links between those records and your personal identity, achieving full privacy anonymization while protecting community data.

3. Important Reminders

  • The account deletion and data anonymization process is completely irreversible. All your custom preferences and non-public profiles will be lost permanently.
  • If you experience any technical difficulties during this process or have questions regarding data deletion, please feel free to reach out to us at our official contact email: lost.buddy.euxlab@gmail.com.
