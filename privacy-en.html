When you use the Service, we may collect the following data based on your usage behavior:

  1. Account and Identity Verification Data:

    When you log into the App via a third-party account such as Apple or Google, we collect the unique identifier (UID), email address, and nickname authorized by that third-party platform.

2. Purposes of Using Personal Data

The data we collect will only be used for the following lawful and specific purposes:

  1. To provide, maintain, and optimize the core map search, case publishing, and sighting matching functions of the App.
  2. To perform user identity verification, ensure account security, and provide account deletion management services.
  3. To process user report incidents and prevent misconduct, fraud, and malicious false reporting.
  4. To conduct anonymized technical data analysis to improve App performance and user experience.

3. Third-Party Service Providers and Data Storage Disclosure

The Service utilizes Firebase cloud platform technology provided by Google (including but not limited to Firestore Database and Cloud Storage) as its core data storage and backend operations environment.

  1. The case profiles, photos, and personal identifiers you publish will be encrypted, transmitted, and stored on Google Firebase servers. The information security protection and storage standards perfectly comply with Google's highest security regulations.
  2. Except as required by laws and regulations, judicial investigations, or as necessary to fulfill the Terms of Service (such as publicly displaying the lost dog photos and contact phone numbers you post to the public), we will never sell, rent, or arbitrarily share your personal data with unrelated third parties.

4. User Rights and Account Deletion Timeline

In accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act, you possess the rights to inquire, review, request copies, supplement, correct, stop the collection/processing/utilization, or request the deletion of your personal data.

  1. Account and Data Deletion:

    Registered users can directly apply for "Account Deletion" through the settings feature within the App at any time.

  2. Specific Processing Timeline:

    Once you formally submit an account deletion request, we will completely remove or irreversibly anonymize your account credentials, personal identifiers, and non-public personal files in the cloud database (Firebase) within 7 business days.

  3. Retention Principle for Historical Reports:

    To maintain the integrity of public historical search records, the lost or sighting cases you previously posted on the map will be retained in an anonymized state and displayed as "Lost Buddy User" after your account is deleted, to safeguard the rights of other community users.

5. Children's Privacy Protection

The App is suitable for the general public, but we do not knowingly or intentionally collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age stipulated by local law). If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such data from our servers.
