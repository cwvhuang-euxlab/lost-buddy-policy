Sighting Reports

Please sign up or log in to your account and tap "Found Dogs". Upload a photo, indicate the coat color and breed, and fill in the current time, location, and characteristics. Once submitted, owners can instantly cross-reference this vital clue through filter criteria.

App Usage & Features

If the upload feels slow, it might be due to a weak network signal or because our system is optimizing the image resolution in the background. Try moving to an area with better reception!

About Lost Buddy

Lost Buddy was created by ChihWei, a user experience designer, and Shen, a user interface designer.

Through our long-term monitoring of lost dog notices, we observed that while owners try their absolute best, they often run into a few common hurdles:

  • Information is scattered across different social media platforms and groups, making it difficult to identify the latest updates or duplicate posts.
  • Clues easily get buried by incoming feeds and messages, causing important updates to disappear from view quickly.
  • Without an effective way to compare information, sighting clues still require manually checking photos, characteristics, and locations one by one.

Therefore, we hope to use better information organization, filtering, and presentation to make the search process more efficient, giving every clue and every effort a better chance to make a difference.

The core search, rescue, and reporting features will remain free.

The last thing we want to do is clutter the golden window of rescue with intrusive ads while owners are anxious and the public is trying to help. Currently, app development and store deployment costs are funded entirely out-of-pocket by us. If operational overhead (like servers and databases) grows significantly down the road, we may introduce voluntary donations to keep it running.

If you find Lost Buddy helpful, please share it with your pet-loving communities, leave us a 5-star rating, or write a review! It serves as an immense encouragement to an independent team like us.

Lost Buddy is still in its early stages, and there are many features we'd love to add as resources allow, including real-time notifications, area-based alerts, support for more photos and videos, and sharing success stories from reunited pets. However, the most practical support you can offer is simply keeping an extra eye out on your daily commute and logging a report if you see a wandering pet.
