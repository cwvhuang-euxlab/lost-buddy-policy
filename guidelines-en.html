Lost Buddy Community Guidelines

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Welcome to Lost Buddy! Our core mission is to help lost dogs return home safely through the power of community, honesty, and mutual aid. To maintain a safe, reliable, and respectful environment, all users (regardless of login status) must adhere to these Community Guidelines when posting content. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of platform abuse or malicious behavior.

1. Encouraged & Positive Conduct

  • Accurate & Honest Reporting: When posting lost pet notices or sighting updates, please provide the most accurate time, location coordinates, distinctive features, and clear photographs possible.
  • Compassionate Collaboration: Treat distressed pet owners with empathy and provide verified, constructive clues to assist in their search.

2. Prohibited Content and Misconduct

The platform will actively intervene and address any content or behavior involving the following violations:

  1. False Reports & Fraud: Maliciously reporting fake missing cases, fabricating sighting locations, or exploiting search notices for scamming, extortion of rewards, or other illegal gains.
  2. Objectionable Content & Media: Uploading text or photos unrelated to dog search, including material that is pornographic, violent, hateful, discriminatory, or otherwise offensive.
  3. Harassment & Commercial Abuse: Using public contact details or report functions to harass other users, or utilizing the platform for commercial advertising, marketing, spam, or scams.
  4. System Interference: Exploiting system vulnerabilities or attempting to disrupt normal platform operations via automated scripts, crawlers, or third-party plug-ins.

3. Content Reporting and Review Mechanism

To safeguard the rights of all community users, the App features a built-in content reporting mechanism. Users can also submit a report via our official support email. Our enforcement protocols are as follows:

  • Upon receiving a user report or email flag, we commit to completing an active case review within 24 hours.
  • Based on the factual results of the review and the severity of the infraction, we will take appropriate and corresponding disciplinary measures (including but not limited to: rejecting publication, immediate removal/takedown of the offending content, restricting account posting privileges, or restricting the associated device from accessing the Service) to maintain community order and platform safety.
